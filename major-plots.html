Major Plots in Jujutsu Kaisen

Explore the key story arcs that define the world of Jujutsu Kaisen, from intense battles to shocking revelations.

Shibuya Incident Arc

The Shibuya Incident is one of the most intense arcs in Jujutsu Kaisen, where the villains launch a coordinated attack to seal Satoru Gojo and unleash chaos in Shibuya.

  • Gojo's sealing by the Prison Realm
  • Yuji's confrontation with Mahito
  • Nobara's fateful battle
  • Sukuna's rampage

Hidden Inventory / Gojo's Past Arc

This arc reveals Gojo's past as a student and his relationship with Geto, showing how he became the strongest sorcerer.

  • Gojo and Geto's mission to protect Riko Amanai
  • The birth of modern Gojo
  • Geto's descent into darkness
  • Toji Fushiguro's role

Culling Game Arc

The deadly battle royale orchestrated by Kenjaku where sorcerers and cursed spirits fight for survival.

  • Introduction of new powerful characters
  • Yuta Okkotsu's return
  • Megumi's pivotal role
  • The true nature of Kenjaku's plan
