Characters

Yuji Itadori

The protagonist with immense physical strength and a kind heart who becomes the vessel for Ryomen Sukuna.

Satoru Gojo

The most powerful Jujutsu Sorcerer, known for his Limitless technique and Six Eyes ability.

Megumi Fushiguro

A skilled sorcerer who uses Shikigami techniques and is from the prestigious Zenin family.

Nobara Kugisaki

A strong-willed sorcerer with a unique hammer-and-nails fighting style and no patience for nonsense.

Ryomen Sukuna

The King of Curses, a fearsome and ancient spirit with unimaginable power residing within Yuji.

Maki Zenin

A non-sorcerer who defies her clan and wields powerful cursed tools with incredible skill.

Panda

An adorable but incredibly strong Cursed Corpse who is actually a third-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High.

Toge Inumaki

A sorcerer who uses cursed speech to command others, speaking only in rice ball ingredients to avoid accidental curses.

Yuta Okkotsu

A special-grade sorcerer with unparalleled potential and Rika's spirit, featured in the prequel movie.

Kento Nanami

A reliable and pragmatic sorcerer with a strong sense of duty who uses a ratio-based technique with his blunt sword.

Hanami

A nature-aligned curse spirit with immense power who seeks to eliminate humanity to restore nature's balance.

Mahito

A sadistic curse spirit capable of manipulating souls, representing the darkness in human hearts.
