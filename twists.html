Jujutsu Kaisen - Major Twists

Plot Twists That Shook Us All

Gojo Gets Sealed

Satoru Gojo, the strongest sorcerer, is sealed during the Shibuya Incident arc. His absence causes massive imbalance in the sorcerer world.

Yuji Dies… Twice

Yuji dies after consuming Sukuna’s finger, only to be resurrected. Later, he dies again—but Sukuna brings him back, deepening their bond.

Megumi's Sister's Fate

Tsumiki Fushiguro, Megumi's sister, awakens as a participant in the Culling Game, and her true identity sends shockwaves through the fandom.
