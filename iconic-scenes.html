Gojo’s Unveiling

Satoru Gojo reveals his Limitless technique and the Six Eyes for the first time in battle, effortlessly overpowering enemies and cementing his status as the strongest sorcerer. The calm confidence and blue glow of his technique made this a defining moment in Jujutsu Kaisen history.

Yuji vs Sukuna

Yuji and Sukuna's internal conflict erupts into a battle of wills and ideologies. The raw power and terrifying potential of Sukuna shows just how dangerous it is to contain the King of Curses within a human vessel.

Nobara vs Mahito

In an emotional and climactic clash, Nobara takes on Mahito’s double, pushing herself to the brink in a fearless showdown. Her fierce resolve and unwavering will made this moment unforgettable and tragic.
